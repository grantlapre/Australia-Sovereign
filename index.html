What courts look for

  • Connection: Is it logically connected to that goal?
  • Necessity: Is there a less restrictive way to achieve the goal?
  • Balance: Is the burden on lawful speech justified by the benefit?

  • Minimise discretion; prefer clear criteria.
  • Include oversight: reporting, independent review, review dates.
